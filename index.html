Justice Martin

I got my start in programming from my father. He was a sofware engineer, and, because of him, I took my first programming class when I was in high school. I enjoyed it so much that I went on to take additional courses, in both high school and college. However, during college I ended up majoring in accounting, and, upon graduation, I entered the workforce as a public accountant.

During my career as an accountant, I found myself utilizing my programming knowledge more and more, in order to automate daily accounting tasks. But, I discovered that I did this because I enjoyed the programming more than the accounting, until, eventually, I decided to make the full career switch to programming.

Now, I know languages like JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS, and I can't wait to learn even more.
